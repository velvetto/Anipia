Rules

  • All animals must have up-to-date vaccinations.
  • Pets should be brought in clean and healthy condition.
  • Owners must provide specific feeding instructions.
  • Any health issues should be communicated in advance.
  • Accommodation is based on the size and nature of the pet.

Services

Our hotel offers a wide range of services for animals, including grooming, walking, and special exercises.

Grooming

This includes activities like bathing, hair care, nail trimming, and skincare.

Walking

This activity helps maintain the pet’s physical health and provides mental stimulation.

Exercise

Regular exercise is crucial for a pet’s overall well-being and happiness.

Feeding

Our hotel offers personalized plans to ensure your pet gets the right diet for their health.

Veterinary Care

Regular check-ups, preventive care, and immediate medical attention if needed.

Playtime

Activities that stimulate your pet's mind and keep them entertained throughout their stay.
